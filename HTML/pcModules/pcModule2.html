The concept of Ground

The word ground kept being mentioned but I didn't know what that was reffering to. I did some independent research reading and watching videos to get my head around it. Essentially, Ground is the return path for electrical current because it is an electrical sink. The charge needs somewhere to go after completing its circuit, which is why it goes to Ground. "True earth ground" refers to physically putting metal in the actual dirt or ground which is where the name comes from.

Potentiometer

A potentiometer is a type of resistor with a control knob similar to a dial. The idea behind this is the adjust the current and be able to variably control electrical components. After our inital build we were getting annoyed with how loud the buzzer was, so we added the potentiometer to restrict its volume.
