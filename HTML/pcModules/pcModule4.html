Sketching and storyboarding

This module intended to collect our previous brainstorming into a more resolved wireframe. Though these are still hand sketches, they provide a more detailed view of what my website might looks like, after having settled on the 'sketchbook' theme. I chose this theme because I felt it was the idea that felt the most natural to integrate progress photos and rough sketches, and had really good scalability to make a website visually appealing, and interactive. Despite this, I would love the opportunity to create something else artistic using HTML, CSS and JS, that might fit better without the documentation requirement.
